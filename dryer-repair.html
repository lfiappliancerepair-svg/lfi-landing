What we fix

Common dryer problems we solve

If your dryer is doing any of these, we can help — usually the same day.

No heat

Clothes come out cold or damp — heating element, thermostat or thermal fuse.

Takes too long

Multiple cycles to dry — clogged vent and airflow restored (and lower bills).

Won’t tumble

Drum won’t turn — belt, roller or motor replaced.

Loud noises

Squealing, thumping or grinding — rollers, belt and bearings.

Won’t start

Dead dryer — door switch, thermal fuse and controls checked.

Overheating

Too hot or shutting off mid-cycle — venting and thermostats inspected for safety.
