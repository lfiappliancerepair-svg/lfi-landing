What we fix

Common refrigerator problems we solve

If your refrigerator is doing any of these, we can help — usually the same day.

Not cooling

Warm fridge or freezer — compressor, thermostat, sensor and sealed-system faults diagnosed precisely.

Leaking water

Water inside or on the floor — usually a blocked defrost drain or a faulty water valve.

Ice buildup

Frost in the freezer or on the back wall — defrost system, door seals and airflow checked.

Strange noises

Buzzing, clicking or knocking from the fan, compressor or evaporator.

Ice maker / water

No ice or no water at the dispenser — valve, line and ice-maker module.

Runs constantly

Never shuts off and bills climbing — thermostat, seals and coils inspected.
