Good to know

Frequently asked questions

How much does a diagnostic visit cost?
We charge $99 for a diagnostic visit, and it’s waived when you proceed with the repair — so if we fix it, the diagnosis is included.
How soon can a technician come out?
Same-day service is available for urgent repairs in most GTA areas. Call early in the day to confirm availability for your area.
What’s included in the warranty?
All repairs come with a warranty on both parts and labour. If the same issue reoccurs within the warranty period, we fix it at no additional cost.
Do I need to buy parts myself?
No — we handle parts selection and sourcing. Just describe the problem and we take care of the rest.
What areas do you serve?
All of Toronto and the GTA — Etobicoke, Mississauga, Scarborough, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Richmond Hill, North York — plus Oakville and Hamilton.
How long does a typical repair take?
Most common repairs are done in 1–2 hours. Jobs needing specific ordered parts may take longer — we’ll give you a clear timeline during the diagnostic visit.
