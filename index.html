What we repair

Most common household appliances. If you don’t see your unit here – send us a message, we’ll let you know if we can help.

Refrigerators & freezers

Not cooling, icing up, water leaks, strange noises – we diagnose the cause and offer repair options.

Washers & dryers

Not spinning, not draining, noisy operation, no heat, error codes – we focus on safe and long-term solutions.

Dishwashers

Poor cleaning, water left in the tub, leaks, strange noises – we locate the root cause, not just reset the unit.

Ovens, stoves & cooktops

No heat, uneven baking, burners not working – electric ranges and built-in ovens.

Microwaves & range hoods

Light on but not heating, fan issues, control problems – we assess whether it’s worth repairing.
