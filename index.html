Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our appliance repair services.

How much does diagnostic service cost? +
Diagnostic visit is $99. This fee is completely waived if you proceed with the repair. We believe you shouldn't pay just to find out what's wrong.
Do you offer same-day service? +
Yes! We offer same-day service for urgent repairs in most areas of Toronto and GTA. Call us early in the day to check availability.
What's included in your warranty? +
We provide 90 days warranty on both parts and labor for all repairs. If the same issue reoccurs within this period, we'll fix it at no additional cost.
Do I need to buy parts myself? +
No, we source all genuine parts for you. We have relationships with suppliers to get quality parts at better prices, and we warranty them as part of our service.
What areas do you serve? +
We serve all of Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area including Etobicoke, Mississauga, Scarborough, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, and Richmond Hill.
How long do most repairs take? +
Most common repairs are completed in 1-2 hours. Complex issues might require ordering specific parts, but we'll give you a clear timeline during diagnostics.
