Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our appliance repair coordination services.

How much does diagnostic coordination cost? +
Contractors in our network typically charge $99 for diagnostic visits. This fee is usually waived if you proceed with the repair through our coordination service.
Do you offer same-day technician connection? +
Yes! We can often connect you with technicians offering same-day service for urgent repairs in most areas of Toronto and GTA. Contact us early in the day to check contractor availability.
What's included in contractor warranty? +
Contractors in our network provide 90 days warranty on both parts and labor for all repairs. If the same issue reoccurs within this period, they'll fix it at no additional cost.
Do I need to buy parts myself? +
No, contractors in our network source all genuine parts for you. They have relationships with suppliers to get quality parts at better prices, and warranty them as part of their service.
What areas do you coordinate services in? +
We coordinate services across all of Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area including Etobicoke, Mississauga, Scarborough, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, and Richmond Hill.
How long do most contractor repairs take? +
Most common repairs are completed in 1-2 hours by contractors in our network. Complex issues might require ordering specific parts, but contractors provide clear timelines during diagnostics.
